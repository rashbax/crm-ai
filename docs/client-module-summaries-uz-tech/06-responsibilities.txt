Sahifa: Responsibilities
Route: `/responsibilities`

Maqsad
SKU ownership matrix. Har bir SKU uchun kim marketplace owner, kim ads, kim content, kim supply, kim reviewer va kim backup ekanini formal ko'rinishda yuritadi.

API va storage
- `GET /api/founder/responsibilities`
- `POST /api/founder/responsibilities`
- `GET /api/founder/audit?entityType=responsibility&entityId=...`
- Storage: `data/secure/responsibilities.json`

Entity fieldlari
- `skuId`
- `marketplace`
- `marketplaceOwnerId`
- `adsOwnerId`
- `contentOwnerId`
- `supplyOwnerId`
- `reviewerId`
- `backupOwnerId`
- `startDate`
- `assignmentStatus`
- `authorityLimit`
- `notes`

Assignment statuslar
- `active`
- `temporary`
- `backup_active`
- `inactive`

GET endpoint hisoblaydigan signal lar
- `noOwnerCount`: 4 ta primary role ham bo'sh bo'lgan assignmentlar
- `noBackupCount`: active assignment ichida backup owner yo'q
- `overloadedUsers`: bir userga 5 tadan ko'p SKU tushgan holat
- `managerWorkload`: har user bo'yicha SKU soni, open incidents va overdue tasklar

Default authority mapping
- marketplace owner: kundalik operatsiya va odatiy tasklar
- ads owner: kelishilgan limit ichida kampaniya va byudjet
- content owner: kontent yangilash va listing tuzatish
- supply owner: stock xavfi bo'yicha standart tasklar
- reviewer: yopilgan ish va muhim o'zgarishni tekshirish
- backup owner: asosiy egasi yo'qligida barcha vakolatlar

UI funksiyalar
- Summary cards
- Overloaded manager signal bloki
- Unassigned SKU shortcutlari
- Role-gap filter: tanlangan role slot bo'sh bo'lgan qatorlarni ko'rsatadi
- Quick filter: no owner, no backup, inactive
- Detail modal
- Edit/create modal
- History modal

Biznes qiymati
- SKU bo'yicha javobgarlik noaniqligini yo'qotadi.
- Backup ownership sabab ta'til yoki yo'qlik paytida jarayon to'xtab qolmaydi.
