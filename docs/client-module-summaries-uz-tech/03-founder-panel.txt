Sahifa: Founder Panel
Route: `/founder-panel`

Maqsad
Founder yoki CEO uchun real-time boshqaruv paneli. U operatsion risk, execution bottleneck, finance, sales trend, team health va AI xulosani bitta ekranda birlashtiradi.

Asosiy data manbalari
- `GET /api/founder/dashboard?marketplace=...`
- `GET /api/orders?marketplace=...`
- `GET /api/products?marketplace=...`
- `GET /api/finance?startDate=...&endDate=...&summaryOnly=1&forceLiveBalance=1`
- `GET /api/ai/summary?lang=...`
- `POST /api/ai/ask`

Widget bloklari
- Critical incidents
- Overdue tasks
- Pending approvals
- Owner biriktirilmagan SKU lar
- Stockout risk SKU lar
- Loss-risk items
- Ad-stock conflicts
- Unanswered appeals
- SLA-breached appeals
- Negative appeals
- Silent incidents
- Expired approvals
- Integration alerts
- Stale tasks

Founder dashboard API hisoblaydigan muhim signallar
- `silentIncidents`: active incident 12 soatdan ko'proq yangilanmagan.
- `expiredApprovals`: `pending` va `expiresAt < now`.
- `staleApprovals`: `pending` va 24 soatdan ko'proq qarorsiz.
- `criticalEscalations`: `priority=critical`, `isOverdue=true`, `status != done`.
- `staleTasksList`: founder dashboard ichida 48+ soat update bo'lmagan tasklar.

Sales va finance bloki
- Quick range: 7, 14, 28 kun.
- Orders va products dataset birinchi yuklangandan keyin cache-state ichida saqlanadi.
- Finance summary fon rejimida alohida fetch qilinadi, shuning uchun UI filter o'zgarishi chartni tezroq yangilaydi.
- Revenue chart `RevenueChart` komponenti orqali ko'rsatiladi.

Alert generator
- Maksimal 12 alert chiqaradi.
- `outOfStock`: active SKU, `onHand <= 0` va `inbound <= 0`.
- `lowOrCriticalSelling`: stock health low/critical va `daysToStockout <= 21`.
- Trend alert order change asosida qo'shiladi.

AI bloklari
- AI short summary: rahbariyat uchun qisqa bullet-xulosa.
- AI Q/A: founder savol beradi, backend AI kontekst bilan javob qaytaradi.

Qo'shimcha bloklar
- Recent blockers
- Recent critical incidents
- Pending approvals list
- Top risky SKUs
- Team scorecard
- Local checklist tasklar (`storage.getTasks()`)

Biznes qiymati
- Founder uchun alohida status-report yig'ish zaruratini kamaytiradi.
- Interventsiya faqat kritik joylarga yo'naltiriladi.
- Operatsion, moliyaviy va customer-risk bir ekranga chiqadi.
