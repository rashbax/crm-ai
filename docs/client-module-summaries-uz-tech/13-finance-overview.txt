Sahifa: Finance Overview
Route: `/finance`

Maqsad
Joriy marketplace context uchun finance summary, signal va accrual breakdown beruvchi bosh sahifa.

API
- `GET /api/finance?startDate=...&endDate=...&marketplace=...`

Snapshot logikasi
- Response ichida `snapshot.status` bo'ladi: `fresh | stale | missing`
- Snapshot `fresh` bo'lmasa page 10 soniyadan keyin qayta poll qiladi
- Maksimal 6 marta retry qiladi

KPI lar
- `currentBalance`
- `totalIncome`
- `totalExpenses`
- `netIncome`
- Previous period bilan delta

Signal bloklari
- `net_income_drop`
- `refund_rate_high`
- `commission_pressure`
- `withdrawal_delay`
- `adjustments_spike`
- Signal kartasi bosilsa foydalanuvchi marketplace finance detail sahifasiga `typeFilter` bilan yuboriladi

Additional insight
- `Why this result` blokida refund, commission, delivery, ads va previous periodga nisbatan profit drop sabablarini bullet ko'rinishida chiqaradi
- Top SKU profit list
- Bottom/problem SKU list

Accrual breakdown
- sales
- refunds
- ozonReward
- deliveryServices
- agentServices
- fboServices
- adsPromotion
- otherServicesFines
- compensations
- withdrawals
- adjustments
- totalAccrued

Biznes qiymati
- Founder va finance manager uchun tezkor moliyaviy holatni beradi.
- Summary sahifadan detail transaction sahifaga signal-based drilldown mavjud.
