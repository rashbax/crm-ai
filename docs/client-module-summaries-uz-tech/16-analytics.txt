Sahifa: Analytics
Route: `/analytics`

Maqsad
Sales performance, cancellation trend va stockout forecast ni analitik ko'rinishda chiqaradi. Reporting emas, action-oriented analytics.

API
- `GET /api/analytics?days=7|28&marketplace=...`

Hisob-kitob qoidalari
- Active revenue cancelled/returned orderlarsiz hisoblanadi
- `estimatedProfit = totalRevenue * (1 - 0.15)`
- `avgCheckTrend`:
- `up` agar current avg check > prev * 1.05
- `down` agar current avg check < prev * 0.95
- `trendDirection`:
- current periodning 1-yarmi va 2-yarmi revenue average lari orasida 10% dan katta farq bo'lsa `up` yoki `down`

Signal qoidalari
- Declining revenue SKU: previous revenue > 1000 va current revenue < previous * 0.8
- Rising cancel SKU: period ichida kamida 5 order va cancel rate > 20%
- Bottom SKU count: eng past revenue dagi mahsulotlar soni

Bloklar
- KPI: revenue, orders, avg check, cancel rate, estimated profit
- Trend signal kartalari
- Orders trend chart
- Top products table
- Bottom products table
- Declining revenue SKU table
- Stockout forecast table

Forecast jadvali
- `dailyForecast`
- `stockRemaining`
- `stockoutInDays`
- `riskLevel`
- `potentialLoss`
- `recommendedReorderQty`
- Quick action: Tasks yoki Incidents sahifasiga o'tish

Biznes qiymati
- Revenue pasayishi, cancellation oshishi va stockout riskini bir sahifada oldindan ko'rsatadi.
- Analitika natijasini to'g'ridan-to'g'ri operatsion harakatga bog'laydi.
