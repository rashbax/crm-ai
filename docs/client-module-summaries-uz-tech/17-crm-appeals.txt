Sahifa: CRM Appeals
Route: `/crm`

Maqsad
Customer murojaatlari, sharhlar, shikoyatlar va buyurtma bo'yicha requestlarni markazlashgan holda yuritadi.

API va storage
- `GET /api/founder/appeals`
- `POST /api/founder/appeals`
- `POST /api/founder/appeals/sync`
- Storage: `data/secure/appeals.json`

Entity modeli
- `appealType`: `savol | sharh | shikoyat | buyurtma`
- `status`: `yangi | jarayonda | kutyapti | javob_berilgan | yopilgan`
- `priority`: `critical | high | medium | low`
- `sentiment`: `positive | neutral | negative`
- `ownerId`
- optional `orderId`, `skuId`
- `tags`
- `slaDeadline`, `slaBreached`
- `replies[]`
- optional `escalatedTo`, `escalatedAt`

SLA qoidalari
- `shikoyat` -> 4 soat
- `buyurtma` -> 12 soat
- `savol` -> 24 soat
- `sharh` negative bo'lsa -> 8 soat
- `sharh` negative bo'lmasa -> 24 soat
- `slaBreached` faqat `yopilgan` yoki `javob_berilgan` bo'lmagan appeal lar uchun tekshiriladi

Status transition qoidalari
- `yangi -> jarayonda | kutyapti`
- `jarayonda -> javob_berilgan | kutyapti`
- `kutyapti -> jarayonda`
- `javob_berilgan -> yopilgan | yangi`
- `yopilgan -> yangi`

Action lar
- Create
- Sync
- Reply: reply yuborilganda status avtomatik `javob_berilgan` bo'ladi
- Update status
- Escalate
- Delete

UI xususiyatlari
- Stat cardlar: yangi, jarayonda, SLA buzilgan, negative, total
- Search, type, status va priority filterlari
- Pagination
- Reply history modal

Biznes qiymati
- Customer communication ni marketplace kabinetlarida tarqoq holatda qoldirmaydi.
- SLA va negative feedback ni founder-level signalga aylantiradi.
