Page: Finance - Ozon Store Economics
Route: /finance/ozon

Purpose
This page is the detailed Ozon finance ledger and economics view. It takes the high-level finance story and breaks it into actual transactions, SKU contribution, and cost patterns for Ozon specifically.

Main features
- Date range filtering for the reporting window.
- Search over transaction descriptions, orders, and SKU-related entries.
- Transaction type filtering for sales, refunds, commissions, withdrawals, and adjustments.
- Deep-link support from summary finance signals through URL filters.

What the page shows
- Sales and returns block.
- Accruals block.
- Total result block with net income, current balance, total income, and total expenses.
- Signal cards for refund pressure, commission pressure, withdrawal delays, and adjustment spikes.
- Top profitable SKUs and problem SKUs for the selected period.
- Transaction ledger with ID, date, type, description/SKU, amount, and balance.
- Pagination for large statement sets.

Business value
- Lets finance and marketplace managers inspect where Ozon profitability is being created or lost.
- Gives a fast path from high-level alarms to the exact transactions behind them.
