Page: Prices
Route: /prices

Purpose
This module is the pricing workspace. It combines market price management, approval control, promotion visibility, and draft-based batch execution.

How pricing is managed
- Pricing is displayed per SKU and per marketplace row.
- The table compares current live values, draft values, margin context, ownership, promo status, approval state, stock risk, recommendations, price index, and last changed date.

Main features
- Signal cards for loss-risk pricing, promo items that are stuck, pending approvals, and unapplied drafts.
- Filters for SKU search, on-sale/not-on-sale state, blocked items, low margin, and high-risk stock.
- Draft queue entry count visible in the page header.

Price editing workflow
- Clicking a row opens the price editor.
- The editor supports new price and discount proposals plus a mandatory business reason.
- Risk warnings appear when discounts cross approval thresholds.
- Proposed changes are added to a draft queue instead of going live immediately.

Approvals and governance
- Approval status is visible per marketplace row.
- Large discount changes can be marked as approval-required.
- Pricing history records old and new values, reasons, operator, and approval outcome.

Additional detail layers
- Promotions modal shows active promo programs and discount depth.
- History modal shows prior price changes.
- Draft modal allows batch review, apply, remove, or clear-all actions.

Business value
- Makes pricing a governed process instead of a hidden manual edit.
- Preserves commercial traceability for founders and managers.
- Supports batch operations while still protecting the business from reckless discounts.
