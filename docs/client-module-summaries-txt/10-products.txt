Purpose
This module is the SKU master registry. It answers three core questions at once: what this SKU is, who owns it, and how healthy or risky it is right now.

Main features
- Summary cards for total SKUs, critical stock, unassigned SKUs, and high-risk SKUs.
- Quick signal filters for low-stock sellers, ownerless SKUs, incident-linked SKUs, overloaded SKUs with many tasks, and pricing-risk SKUs.
- Filtering by search, product status, stock health, risk level, and owner.
- Paginated table with operational metrics per SKU.

Table content
- Current price, stock on hand, inbound stock, 30-day sales, stock health, owner, open tasks, open incidents, risk level, and product status.
- Visual badges for critical or low stock, high risk, and missing ownership.

Detail modal
- Current SKU overview and status.
- Owner information.
- Linked open tasks.
- Linked open incidents.
- Price history timeline.
- Audit/change history.

Business value
- Acts as the operational single source of truth for the catalog.
- Helps teams move from reactive problem solving to proactive SKU management.
- Connects tasks, incidents, pricing, and ownership around the same product record.
