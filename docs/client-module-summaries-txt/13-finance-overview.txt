Page: Finance Overview
Route: /finance

Purpose
This page is the finance summary center for the currently selected marketplace context. It gives leadership a fast answer to: how much came in, how much went out, what balance is available, and what financial risks need attention.

Main features
- Snapshot freshness indicator that tells whether the data is fresh, updating, or missing.
- KPI cards for current balance, period income, period expenses, and net income.
- Comparison against the previous period for income and net profit.
- Date range controls with apply action.

Financial intelligence
- Signal cards for profit drop, high refund rate, commission pressure, withdrawal delay, and adjustment spikes.
- Clicking a finance signal opens the detailed marketplace finance screen already filtered to the relevant transaction type.
- "Why this result" panel explains the most likely drivers of the current financial outcome, such as refunds, commissions, delivery costs, ad spend, or profit deterioration versus the previous period.

Performance detail
- Top SKUs by net contribution.
- Problem SKUs with weak or negative net contribution.
- Full accrual breakdown covering sales, refunds, marketplace reward, delivery services, agent services, FBO services, ads and promotion, fines and other services, compensations, withdrawals, adjustments, and total accrued.

Business value
- Gives a founder-ready financial snapshot without opening raw statements first.
- Helps users move from reading numbers to understanding the reasons behind them.
- Connects summary finance with deeper Ozon or Wildberries transaction analysis in one click.
