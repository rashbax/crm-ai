Purpose
This module is the formal register for operational problems and business risks. It is designed so serious issues are recorded, owned, tracked against SLA, and either resolved or escalated properly.

Incident model
- Incidents are typed and prioritized by severity.
- Creation requires a title, owner, due date, root cause, and action plan.
- Optional fields include reviewer, estimated financial loss, marketplace, and SKU.

Main features
- Status counters for Open, In Progress, Waiting, Reopened, Resolved, and Closed.
- Additional signal counters for critical incidents, silent incidents, and escalated incidents.
- Warning banners for incidents without owners and incidents with no recent updates.
- Search and severity filtering.

Operational workflow
- Structured incident creation with mandatory problem analysis.
- Status transitions through the life cycle.
- Escalate/de-escalate control for founder attention.
- Reopen flow with a mandatory reason when a supposedly solved issue returns.
- Delete action for active incidents that were created by mistake.

Table and detail behavior
- Table shows severity, reopen count, title, type, owner, reviewer, status, and SLA state.
- Visual badges show escalation, silence, and repeated reopening.
- Detail modal shows full metadata, root cause, action plan, financial impact, reopen history, and linked task IDs.

Business value
- Prevents important operational failures from being lost in chat or spreadsheets.
- Adds root-cause discipline instead of superficial status updates.
- Gives management a measurable incident process with ownership and response timing.
