Page: Founder Panel
Route: /founder-panel

Purpose
This is the executive control dashboard for the founder or CEO. It combines risk monitoring, operational escalation, finance, sales trends, team performance, and AI-generated summaries into one decision screen.

What the page shows
- Signal cards for the most urgent business issues: critical incidents, overdue tasks, pending approvals, SKUs without owners, stockout risk, loss-risk items, ad-versus-stock conflicts, unanswered appeals, SLA-breached appeals, negative appeals, silent incidents, expired approvals, integration alerts, and stale tasks.
- Short operational lists for blocked tasks, critical incidents, and approvals waiting for leadership decisions.
- A dedicated escalation table for critically overdue tasks that need founder intervention.
- A risky SKU block that ranks products by stockout exposure, incident load, ownership gaps, and risk score.
- A stale-task block that surfaces work with no recent updates.
- KPI cards for on-time execution, overdue workload, incident resolution speed, approval delays, founder interventions, and stockout cases.
- Team scorecards with active work, overdue work, blocked work, incidents, risky SKUs, interventions, and update freshness for each responsible person.

Finance and sales section
- Current balance and accrued amount for the selected period.
- Revenue and order trend chart with quick ranges for 7, 14, and 28 days.
- Average check and average daily order pace.
- Period-based filtering for leadership review.

Automated intelligence
- Built-in alert generation based on stock position and sales behavior.
- AI short summary block that refreshes executive highlights.
- AI question-and-answer block where leadership can ask follow-up business questions directly from the dashboard.

Additional utilities
- Quick daily task checklist area for founder-level follow-up items.
- Deep links from signal cards into Tasks, Incidents, Approvals, Products, CRM Appeals, and Settings.

Business value
- Replaces fragmented status reporting with one live control room.
- Makes leadership bottlenecks visible immediately.
- Helps the founder intervene only where it matters most.
