Purpose
This module is the SKU ownership matrix. It defines exactly who is responsible for every product and every role around that product.

Responsibility model
- One SKU assignment can include a marketplace owner, ads owner, content owner, supply owner, reviewer, and backup owner.
- Each assignment also stores a start date, assignment status, authority limit, and optional notes.

Main features
- Summary cards for total assigned SKUs, SKUs without owners, SKUs without backups, and products that are still unassigned.
- Signal block for overloaded managers whose workload is too large.
- Quick assignment shortcuts for SKUs that exist in Products but do not yet have owners.
- Manager workload cards showing SKU count, open incidents, and overdue tasks by person.
- Search, role-gap filtering, and quick filters for no-owner, no-backup, and inactive assignments.

Table behavior
- Shows SKU, marketplace, assignment status, start date, all role holders, reviewer, backup, and actions.
- Highlights missing owners or missing backup coverage visually.
- Opens detail, edit, history, and delete actions from each row.

Detail and editing
- Detail modal shows all role assignments plus authority limits and notes.
- Create/edit modal supports structured assignment setup, including temporary or inactive statuses.
- History modal shows full audit tracking for assignment changes.

Business value
- Removes ambiguity over who owns what.
- Supports continuity through backup assignment, not just a single owner.
- Helps leadership identify staffing gaps, overloaded managers, and SKUs that are operationally exposed.
