Page: CRM Appeals
Route: /crm

Purpose
This module manages customer-facing appeals, reviews, complaints, and order-related requests. It acts as the communication registry for inbound marketplace issues.

Appeal model
- Supports appeal type, status, priority, sentiment, owner, marketplace, optional order ID, optional SKU, and tags.
- Includes SLA monitoring so time-sensitive cases do not sit without response.

Main features
- Stats cards for new appeals, in-progress appeals, SLA-breached appeals, negative-sentiment appeals, and total appeals.
- Search plus filtering by appeal type, status, and priority.
- Global marketplace context inherited from the top bar.
- Pagination for long appeal queues.

Operational actions
- Sync action to import marketplace feedback or appeal items.
- Manual appeal creation modal for non-imported cases.
- Reply modal with reply history and structured reply outcome.
- Status transition modal based on allowed workflow steps.
- Escalation action for high-priority or critical cases.
- Delete action for invalid or obsolete records.

Table behavior
- Highlights SLA-breached and newly arrived cases.
- Shows customer, message preview, owner, SLA state, status, tags, and escalation markers.

Business value
- Gives the business a structured way to respond to marketplace-facing customer issues.
- Helps protect SLA, reputation, and owner accountability.
- Connects customer pain points back to operational owners instead of leaving them in external portals only.
