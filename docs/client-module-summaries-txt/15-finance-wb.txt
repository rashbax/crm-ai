Page: Finance - Wildberries Store Economics
Route: /finance/wb

Purpose
This page is the Wildberries-specific finance ledger and economics view. It mirrors the detailed finance workflow of the Ozon page but isolates Wildberries transactions and contribution.

Main features
- Date range filtering for the reporting window.
- Search and transaction-type filtering.
- Signal-driven deep links from the summary finance page.
- Summary area for sales, returns, accruals, total financial result, current balance, income, and expenses.
- Top profitable SKUs and problem SKUs.
- Transaction ledger with full pagination.

What makes it useful
- Helps the team separate channel-level performance instead of mixing all marketplaces together.
- Makes Wildberries commission, refund, and adjustment patterns visible at the statement level.

Business value
- Supports marketplace-level profitability control.
- Useful for finance review, founder audits, and category-level decision making on Wildberries.
